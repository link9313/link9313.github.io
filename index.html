The Houses of Congress

A game about the US Government. You are a member of the house or senate of either major party. You vote on the issues and score points after being reelected by your voters. I made it for my US Government class in 11th grade. I wrote in FreeBASIC and using FB GUI.

Calculus Graph

A program that displays various graphs and calculates the integrals with GSL 1.8. I made this for my Calculus A/B class in 12th grade. I made it in Visual C++ 2010.

Emmerse

A virtual reality system for the Oculus Rift DK2 designed to display videos in 360-degrees. Designed with agile development for a class project in the Applied Innovations course at The University of Nebraska at Omaha.

GitHub Repo

Nebraska Watershed Network

A project for the Nebraska Watershed Network operating out of The University of Nebraska at Omaha to assist with data collection from citizen scientists. Built with CakePHP and Bootstrap. Mobile applications were designed by other teams in a whole class project for the Agile Devlopment Methods course at The University of Nebraska at Omaha. I led the team in the use of the SCRUM agile development system.
